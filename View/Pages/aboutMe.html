About Me

My name is Lucia, and I am a highly motivated and self-driven individual with a strong passion for learning and adapting to new environments. I hold an MRes in Biosciences (Distinction) from Aberystwyth University and a BSc in Marine and Freshwater Biology (Upper Second-Class Honours, 2:1). Throughout my academic journey, I have developed excellent time management and organizational skills, enabling me to multitask effectively and meet multiple deadlines with precision.


My experience in scientific research has equipped me with the ability to collect, record, and analyze field and laboratory data efficiently. I have honed my skills in handling large datasets in an organized manner and presenting findings using Microsoft Office, DNASP, PopArt, Mega, and R for map creation. Additionally, I have gained experience in writing consultancy and scientific reports, demonstrating my keen attention to detail and ability to convey complex information clearly.


Beyond my academic and professional pursuits, I have a variety of interests that reflect my love for creativity and exploration. Originally from Portugal, I relocated to the UK, where I continue to embrace new experiences. I am passionate about reading, as it allows me to expand my knowledge and unwind. I also enjoy baking, finding it both a relaxing hobby and a creative outlet for experimenting with different recipes. Spending time outdoors, whether hiking, exploring nature, or simply enjoying the fresh air, is another way I recharge and maintain a healthy balance in life.


With a strong commitment to continuous learning and excellence, I am eager to apply my skills and adaptability to new challenges and opportunities.
