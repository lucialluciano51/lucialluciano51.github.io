About Me

Hi, my name is Lúcia and I am a highly motivated and self-driven individual. I also enjoy learning new skills and I am determined to adapt to new environments. I have a passion for the marine environment which led me to study Marine and Freshwater Biology (BSc) and Biosciences (MRes) at Aberystwyth Univerisity. I have always had an eye for detail and I do love organisation. Throughout my academic journey I have developed excellent time management skills which, along with my love for organisation, allows me to multi-task effectively and meet multiple deadlines with precision.


My experience in scientific research has equipped me with the ability to collect, record and analyse both field and laboratory data. During this time, I have sharpened my skills in handling large data sets in an organised manner and I have learnt to analyse and present findings using Microsoft Office, DNASP, PopArt, Mega, and R for map creation. Additionally I have gained extensive experience writing reports to reveal each study’s findings, demonstrating my keen attention for detail and ability to convey complex information clearly.


Beyond my academic and professional pursuits, I have many interests that reflect my passion for creativity and exploration. I am originally from Alentejo, Portugal, a region known for its slow way of living as well as its views and wildlife. I relocated to the UK as a teenager, where I have been embracing new experiences. I enjoy spending time outdoors, whether it is hiking, exploring nature or simply enjoying the weather. I love finding new hobbies, like drawing, painting and crochet. However, I do find that baking is a favourite of mine, as it allows me to both relax and unwind as well as be creative while experimenting with different recipes.


With a strong commitment to continuous learning and excellence, I am eager to apply my skills and adaptability to new challenges and opportunities.
