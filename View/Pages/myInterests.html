My Interests

I enjoy baking and the process of turning simple ingredients into something tasty and comforting. I also appreciate spending time outdoors, whether it’s walking in nature or just taking in the fresh air. Reading is another interest of mine; I like exploring different genres and learning from a variety of perspectives. I’ve always had a soft spot for animals, too—they add a sense of warmth and connection to life. These interests keep me balanced and provide a nice mix of creativity, relaxation, and curiosity.
